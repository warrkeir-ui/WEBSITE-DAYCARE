            <h1>Pumba Classroom (Preschool)</h1>

            <p>The Pumba classroom prepares children for kindergarten by focusing on school readiness skills. Our curriculum is designed to foster a love of learning and build a strong academic foundation.</p>

            <h2>Curriculum Agenda</h2>

            <p>Our preschool curriculum includes:</p>

            <ul>

                <li><strong>Literacy:</strong> Phonics, letter recognition, and early reading skills.</li>

                <li><strong>Numeracy:</strong> Counting, number recognition, and basic math concepts.</li>

                <li><strong>Science and Social Studies:</strong> Hands-on experiments and exploring the world around them.</li>

                <li><strong>Social-Emotional Development:</strong> Learning to cooperate, solve conflicts, and express feelings.</li>

                <li><strong>Creative Arts:</strong> Music, art, and dramatic play to encourage self-expression.</li>

            </ul>
