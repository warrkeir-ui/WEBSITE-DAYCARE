            <h1>Timon Classroom (Toddler)</h1>

            <p>In the Timon classroom, we focus on helping toddlers explore their world with a sense of wonder and independence. Our curriculum supports their growing curiosity and physical abilities.</p>

            <h2>Curriculum Agenda</h2>

            <p>Our toddler curriculum focuses on key developmental milestones, including:</p>

            <ul>

                <li><strong>Independent Exploration:</strong> Allowing children to choose their own activities and build confidence.</li>

                <li><strong>Social Skills:</strong> Learning to share, take turns, and interact with peers.</li>

                <li><strong>Language Development:</strong> Expanding vocabulary through stories, songs, and conversations.</li>

                <li><strong>Fine and Gross Motor Skills:</strong> Activities like stacking blocks, drawing, and running and jumping to build coordination.</li>

                <li><strong>Problem-Solving:</strong> Puzzles and simple cause-and-effect activities.</li>

            </ul>
